Arma3 is a military tactical shooter.

Republic of Altis and Stratis (Altis and Stratis are fictional islands in the Mediterranean Sea) was formed at the end of the 20th century when it gained independence from the British. In 2026, Colonel Georgious Akhanteros launched a coup and took over. Civil war ensued. There was some resistance offered by the Loyalists, people who wanted to restore the original government. However, they were decisively defeated and Jerusalem Cease Fire agreement marked the end of the civil war.
